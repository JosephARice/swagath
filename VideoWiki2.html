Collaborative Video Wiki Platform Ideas

Content Structure

  • Dynamic Channels: Introduce "smart" channels where content is automatically categorized using AI-based tagging and keywords.
  • Intuitive Navigation: Implement visual maps or flowcharts to represent the hierarchy of main topics and subtopics.
  • Personalized Feeds: Allow users to follow topics or creators for curated content recommendations tailored to their interests.

Contribution Features

  • Advanced Editing Tools: Provide drag-and-drop functionality for easy timeline editing, filters, transitions, and voiceover options.
  • Crowdsourced Annotation: Enable community-driven timestamp annotations with peer review.
  • Interactive Videos: Allow creators to add clickable elements like quizzes, links, or branching storylines to videos.

Quality Control

  • AI-Powered Content Review: Integrate tools to identify inappropriate or low-quality content for moderator review.
  • Reputation-Based Roles: Users' contributions and peer reviews
